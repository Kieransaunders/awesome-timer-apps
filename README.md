## Pomodoro & Focus Timers

Apps built around structured work intervals and break cycles.

- **[iTimedIT](https://itimedit.com)** — Cross-platform timer app for individuals and teams. Tracks time across tasks and projects with a clean, distraction-free interface. Available on iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows.
- **[Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc)** — Gamified focus timer that grows a virtual tree during work sessions. Pairs with real tree planting. iOS and Android.
- **[Flow](https://www.flow.app)** — Minimalist Pomodoro timer for Mac with iCal integration, focus/break tracking, and 100k+ daily users.
- **[Session](https://www.stayinsession.com)** — Deep work timer for Apple platforms with goal tracking, music integration, and detailed productivity stats.
- **[Focus To-Do](https://www.focustodo.cn)** — Combines Pomodoro timers with a full to-do list manager. Cross-platform including Apple Watch. 200 million+ hours tracked.
- **[Pomofocus](https://pomofocus.io)** — Clean, browser-based Pomodoro timer. No login required. Minimal and fast.
- **[PomoDone](https://pomodoneapp.com)** — Pomodoro layer that sits on top of existing tools like Trello, Asana, and Todoist.
- **[Reclaim.ai Pomodoro Timer](https://reclaim.ai/pomodoro-timer)** — Free Pomodoro timer from Reclaim, with optional AI calendar scheduling and Slack status sync.

## Time Tracking Apps

Apps focused on logging time across projects, clients, or tasks.

- **[Toggl Track](https://toggl.com/track)** — Industry-standard time tracker for freelancers and teams. Integrates with Trello, Asana, and more. Has a built-in Pomodoro mode.
- **[Clockify](https://clockify.me)** — Free time tracker with unlimited users. Web, desktop, and mobile. Popular for agencies and freelancers.
- **[Harvest](https://www.getharvest.com)** — Time tracking with invoicing and project budgeting. Widely used by agencies.
- **[RescueTime](https://www.rescuetime.com)** — Automatic time tracking that runs in the background and categorises how you spend your time.

## Cross-Platform & Team Timers

- **[iTimedIT](https://itimedit.com)** — Built for individuals and small teams needing time tracking across devices including watchOS.
- **[Hubstaff](https://hubstaff.com)** — Time tracking for remote teams with screenshots, GPS, and payroll integration.
- **[Time Doctor](https://www.timedoctor.com)** — Employee time tracking with productivity monitoring and integrations.

## Open Source

- **[Goodtime](https://github.com/adrcotfas/Goodtime)** — Open-source, ad-free Pomodoro timer for Android. Lightweight with detailed stats and label support.
- **[ActivityWatch](https://activitywatch.net)** — Open-source automatic time tracker. Privacy-first, runs locally.
